UI Internship Program

We are currently seeking a motivated and talented beginner UI designer to join our team for for a 3-month internship program.

UI Internship Program - ProCohat

As an intern at ProCohat, you will have the opportunity to work alongside our experienced Designers and Developers to help create compelling and cohesive brands for our clients.

You will gain hands-on experience in all aspects of the design process, from researching and conceptualizing to creating final designs and presenting them to clients.

Not only that, you will also receive an Internship Certificate and an Experience Certificate upon completion of the program.

Here are some of the responsibilities you will have as a UI design intern at ProCohat:

  • Helping to create Logic Diagrams, User Flow, Wireframes and Mockups for clients' websites and mobile apps.
  • Collaborating with the design team to develop and refine visual concepts.
  • Learning about user-centered design principles and how to apply them in your work.
  • Learning about Brand Design and the Psychology of Design.
  • Assisting with the creation of brand guidelines and visual assets.
  • Participating in team meetings and presentations.

We are looking for someone who:

  • Has a desire to learn about UI design, and has a passion for design and an eye for detail.
  • Has some experience using design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, or Figma
  • Strong problem solving and intellectual thinking skills.
  • A learner mindset, who is always eager to learn and grow.
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills.

The internship program is unpaid, however the payment will be decided based on your input and contribution during the program.

Based on your performance during the internship, there is an opportunity to get a permanent job in the company.
